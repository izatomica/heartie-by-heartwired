# The 3-Layer Framework

> Heartwired's intellectual foundation and signature approach

## The Foundational Concept

Heartwired is built on a **3-layer marketing framework** that separates strategy from tactics. This is the intellectual foundation of the entire platform and Iza's signature approach.

Most solopreneurs jump straight to tactics (posting, emailing, creating content) without the strategic foundation. They're busy but not effective. Heartwired fixes this by guiding users to build from the bottom up.

**The Key Insight:** You can't write effective content (Layer 3) without knowing your voice and channels (Layer 2). You can't choose channels without knowing who you're talking to (Layer 1). Most marketing fails because people skip layers.

## Framework Integration Points

The 3-layer framework should appear in:

1. **Onboarding** - Explain the concept early, organize categories by layer
2. **Strategy Page** - Visual diagram showing layers and completion
3. **Dashboard** - "Framework Health" card showing layer progress
4. **AI Generation** - Show which layers inform content quality
5. **Heartie Messages** - Reference layers when guiding users
6. **Empty States** - Remind users to build foundation first

## The "Aha Moment"

Design for this realization:

**Before Heartwired:**
"I'm posting every day but nothing's working."

**After understanding the framework:**
"Oh! I was doing Layer 3 without Layer 1. No wonder my content felt random."

## Why This Matters

### The Problem with Skipping Layers

**Scenario 1: Starting with Layer 3 (Most Common Mistake)**
- User: "I'll post on LinkedIn, Instagram, and TikTok every day!"
- Problem: No idea who they're talking to, what makes them different, or what their voice is
- Result: Generic content, no engagement, burnout

**Scenario 2: Starting with Layer 2 (Also Common)**
- User: "I'll be on all the platforms where my audience is!"
- Problem: Don't know who their audience actually is or what they need
- Result: Scattered presence, no clear message

**The Right Way: Layer 1 â†’ Layer 2 â†’ Layer 3**
- Layer 1: "I serve burnt-out corporate women who want to start coaching businesses"
- Layer 2: "I'll use LinkedIn (where they are) with an encouraging, 'you can do this' voice"
- Layer 3: "I'll post 3x/week about burnout recovery, with 1 CTA to my free guide"
- Result: Focused, effective, sustainable

## How Heartie Enforces the Framework

### Progressive Disclosure
- Strategy view shows all 7 categories grouped by layer
- Completion tracking per layer
- Recommended starting point (Layer 1)
- Visual hierarchy shows foundation-first approach

### Gentle Guidance
- Heartie suggests completing Layer 1 before Layer 2
- Dashboard shows framework health
- Empty states remind users of missing foundation
- AI generation quality improves with completed layers

### But Not Rigid
- Users CAN skip layers if they want
- No hard gates or forced flows
- Encouragement, not enforcement
- Respects user autonomy
