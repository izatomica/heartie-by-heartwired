# Strategic Categories

> The 7 categories that make up Heartwired's framework

## Overview

Heartie organizes marketing strategy into **7 strategic categories** across the 3 layers. Each category has:
- An emoji icon for visual recognition
- Quick version (surface-level questions)
- Deep version (comprehensive exploration)
- Progress tracking
- Completion percentage

## Layer 1: Brand Strategy (Foundation)
**WHO you serve & WHY you matter**

### üéØ Your Customer
**What it is:** Who you're really talking to

**Why it matters:** You can't market effectively if you don't know who you're talking to. Generic messaging speaks to no one.

**Key Questions:**
- Who is your ideal customer?
- What are they struggling with?
- What do they want to achieve?
- Where are they in their journey?
- What language do they use?

**Quick vs. Deep:**
- Quick: Basic demographics and pain points
- Deep: Psychographics, journey mapping, decision-making process

---

### üìç Your Position
**What it is:** What makes you different

**Why it matters:** In a crowded market, you need a clear reason why someone should choose you over alternatives.

**Key Questions:**
- What makes you unique?
- Who are you NOT for?
- What's your specific angle or approach?
- What do you believe that others don't?
- What can you deliver that others can't?

**Quick vs. Deep:**
- Quick: Basic differentiation and niche
- Deep: Competitive analysis, unique methodology, proof points

---

### üí¨ Your Messaging
**What it is:** The words that connect

**Why it matters:** Even with clarity on customer and position, you need language that resonates and converts.

**Key Questions:**
- What's your core message?
- What transformation do you offer?
- What's your tagline or elevator pitch?
- What stories illustrate your value?
- What objections do you need to address?

**Quick vs. Deep:**
- Quick: Basic value proposition and elevator pitch
- Deep: Messaging framework, storytelling angles, objection handling

---

- What are your non-negotiables?

**WHAT you do & WHEN you do it**

### üöÄ Your Big Plans
**What it is:** Quarterly goals, launches, campaigns

**Why it matters:** Tactical execution needs strategic direction. Quarterly planning provides focus without rigidity.

**Key Questions:**
- What are your big goals this quarter?

## Category Completion & Progress

### Tracking Completion
- Each category shows completion percentage (0-100%)
- Based on questions answered
- Visual progress bar in Strategy view
- Dashboard shows overall framework health

### Quick vs. Deep Versions
**Quick Version:**
- 3-5 core questions
- 15-20 minutes to complete
- Gets essential information
- Good for getting started

**Deep Version:**
- 10-15 comprehensive questions
- 45-60 minutes to complete
- Thorough exploration
- Recommended for Layer 1 categories

**Switching Versions:**
- Users can toggle between Quick and Deep
- Answers transfer where questions overlap
- Can start with Quick, upgrade to Deep later

## Category Flow in UI

### Strategy View
- All 7 categories displayed
- Grouped by layer (visual hierarchy)
- Progress shown per category
- Recommended starting point indicated
- Click to expand category

### Category Detail View
- Version toggle (Quick/Deep)
- Multi-section questionnaire
- Save progress
- Section-by-section navigation
- Completion status
