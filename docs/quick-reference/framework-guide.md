# Quick Reference: The 3-Layer Framework

> One-page summary of Heartwired's core philosophy

## The Key Insight

**You can't write effective content (Layer 3) without knowing your voice and channels (Layer 2). You can't choose channels without knowing who you're talking to (Layer 1). Most marketing fails because people skip layers.**

Most solopreneurs jump straight to tactics (posting, emailing, creating content) without the strategic foundation. They're busy but not effective. Heartwired fixes this by guiding users to build from the bottom up.
